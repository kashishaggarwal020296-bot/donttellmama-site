Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Don't Tell Mama ("we," "us," "our") operates donttellmama.in. This page explains what information we collect through this website and how we use it.

What We Collect

When you make a reservation enquiry, submit The Commons event enquiry form, or join a waitlist through this site, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Details you provide about your visit or event (date, time, headcount, occasion)

We do not require an account, email address, or payment information to use this website.

How We Use It

We use the information you provide to:

  • Contact you by phone or WhatsApp to confirm or discuss your reservation or event enquiry
  • Maintain an internal record of guest visits and enquiries so our team can serve you better on future visits
  • Send occasional updates about new offerings (such as The Commons Pass) — only if you've explicitly opted in via the relevant checkbox

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

WhatsApp Communication

If you contact us or submit an enquiry through this site, we may reach out via WhatsApp to confirm details or follow up. You can ask us to stop contacting you at any time by telling us directly.

Data Storage

Enquiry and reservation information is stored securely in our internal systems and is accessible only to authorized Don't Tell Mama staff involved in guest services and event coordination.

Your Rights

You can ask us to update or delete your information at any time by contacting us directly — see below.

Cookies & Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site (such as which pages are viewed and which buttons are clicked). This helps us improve the website. Analytics data is anonymized and is not linked to your name or phone number.
